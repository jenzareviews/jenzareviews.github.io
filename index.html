Learn About Your Professors at AUA

JenzaReviews is a new and improved way of learning about professors’ teaching styles and course effectiveness. Since this tool is in its early stages, please support us by leaving a few reviews about your past professors! (Note: for now, you must leave at least 1 review to access other reviews.)

This website was created to give students a platform to anonymously read and write feedback about AUA professors. Please remember to be respectful and avoid personal attacks. Inappropriate reviews will be taken down.

Reminder: Reviews reflect individual student experiences and opinions. Your experience with a professor may differ, so don’t let these reviews alone decide how you feel. Use them as a guide, not objective truth.

Disclaimer

JenzaReviews is an independent, student-run platform and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to AUA or its faculty.

All reviews reflect individual student opinions and experiences only and should not be interpreted as factual claims. Users are solely responsible for the content they submit.

Our Policies

  • All reviews must be respectful. Personal attacks or offensive language are not allowed.
  • Reviews must not contain false statements presented as fact, accusations of illegal or unethical behavior, or claims unrelated to a professor’s teaching or course effectiveness.
  • Only AUA students with a verified university email (@edu.aua.am) may submit reviews.
  • Reviews are anonymous to other users, but the system tracks accounts to prevent abuse.
  • Each student can submit at most 1 review per professor to maintain fairness.
  • Administrators reserve the right to remove reviews that violate these rules.
